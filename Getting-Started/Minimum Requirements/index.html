    Minimum Requirements

    PC Only

    FiveM is not available to play on console

    FiveM has similar minimum requirements to GTA V and requires that you have GTA V installed on your computer.

    • GTA V Installed

      You'll need to have a copy of GTA V already installed on your PC. If you don't already own GTA V you can purchase it from Steam, Epic, Rockstar, etc.

      It must be a legally owned copy, and you will need Rockstar Social account

    • FiveM Installed

      FiveM needs to be installed on your PC

      Install FiveM

    • Meet system requirements

      Your PC will need to meet the minimum requirements set by GTA V & FiveM

      See Requirements

    • Applied for entry

      You'll need to apply for access to the server
