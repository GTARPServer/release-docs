    Known Issues

    As a highly customized FiveM RP server, we certainly have our fair share of bugs to go along with it.

    Anytime you come across a bug, please report it to us by using the /report command in the game.

    These are the known issues we're currently aware of, and a workaround, if available. You can also view the recently resolved issues at the bottom.

    General Issues

    Bug Workaround
    No way to get healed at the hospital Purchase bandages from a 24/7 to heal.
    Promo codes may occasionally error when applying No workaround known.
    Using "Revive at Hospital" is not actually a hospital Revive on the ground instead.
    Slight UI Overlap in the pause menu No workaround known (menu is fully functional)
    When spawning in, your spawn camera may start under the map. Wait for the animation to complete
    Your microphone may become stuck always on Set a secondary key bind in the GTA V Settings for Voice Chat.

    Housing Issues

    Bug Workaround
    Placing items in your garden will not show until logout. Log out and then back into the server.

    Police Related Issues

    Bug Workaround
    Trying to view starter apartments in MDT will crash it. Don't click on the property called "{label}"
    Bank / cash register robberies will have an entry in the MDT, but no dispatch notification is triggered Check the MDT manually using /mdt
    Animations do not play in Camera recordings during robberies No workaround known
    When issuing a charge via the MDT, it may be slow. Do not keep clicking as this will stack.

    EMS / Ambulance Related Issues

    Bug Workaround
    There are currently no EMS scripts in the game. RP in chat

    Recently Resolved Issues¶

    If your issue is listed here and you're still experiencing it, please clear your local caches. Instructions here and re-load into the server.
